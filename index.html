Intel Transformation Story

Intel’s decline in traditional PC processors forced the company to reinvent itself. The transformation strategy focuses on three pillars: AI computing, data-center infrastructure, and semiconductor foundry services.

Massive investments in fabrication plants and advanced nodes are intended to restore process leadership while enabling Intel to manufacture chips for external customers.
